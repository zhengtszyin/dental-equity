New York City dental access

Basic dental hygiene should be within reach.

Dental Equity collects sealed, unused oral hygiene supplies and gets them to NYC students, families, shelter residents, and community programs that need them.

Donate Supplies See How It Works
Not everyone has consistent access to basic oral hygiene supplies.

For many students, families, and shelter residents, items like toothpaste, toothbrushes, and floss are not always easy to replace. Dental Equity helps close that gap by moving unused supplies from people and places that have extra to community partners that can distribute them with care.
