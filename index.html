New York City dental access

Making everyday oral care easier to access.

Dental Equity collects sealed, unused oral hygiene supplies and gets them to NYC students, families, shelter residents, and community programs that need them.

Donate Supplies See How It Works
Not everyone has consistent access to basic oral hygiene supplies.

For many students, families, and shelter residents, items like toothpaste, toothbrushes, and floss are not always easy to replace. Dental Equity helps close that gap by moving unused supplies from people and places that have extra to community partners that can distribute them with care.

What We Do

We collect sealed, unused dental hygiene products from across NYC.

Every donation starts as something simple and becomes part of a more reliable care routine.

Dental clinics Extra unopened supplies from care teams.
Businesses Office drives and workplace giving.
Schools Student-led collections and community support.
Community donors Neighbors helping neighbors across NYC.

Where Supplies Go

  • Under-resourced schools
  • Shelters
  • Youth centers
  • Food pantries
  • Community organizations

Community first

Small essentials can make a daily routine possible.

A fresh toothbrush, toothpaste, and floss are simple items, but they support confidence, health, school readiness, and dignity.
